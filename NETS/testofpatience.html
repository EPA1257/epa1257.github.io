a test of patience: a negative extinct tire film


A Test of Patience is unrelated to season 2 of Negative Extinct Tire Show. It is its own baby entirely. I created it as my final film for my film production class. It runs for about two and a half hours.

I was able to get my friends to help by adding some segments to the film. I thank them and everyone in the Oil the Boiled Ball chat for their help with the final product.
