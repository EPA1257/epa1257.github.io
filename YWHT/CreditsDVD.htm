DVD information

The DVD autoplays all tracks in 5.1 when inserted into the player. This is in case you have a fancy DVD-Audio player (in which case I envy you) or if you cannot be bothered to hop through menus. 2.0 files are also present on the disc, and can be accessed by going through the aforementioned menus.
