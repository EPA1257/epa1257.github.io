A Test of Patience: A Negative Extinct Tire Film (2021)

 My high school film class final project, our film was supposed to be a minimum of 2~3 minutes with no set maximum, so with the help of some friends from around the world, I made a nearly 3-hour behemoth, the entirety of which was presented to the class whether they liked it or not.

Negative Extinct Tire Show (2020-2021)

 NETS was an experiment in patience. It had the exact opposite effect that I thought it would have, and I couldn't be happier, really. I think we scared a lot of people. Originally intended as a public access show, the deal fell through and we ended up making it as a school project with full support of our teacher. We fucked around and found out. It was very fun, and seems to be somewhat of a legendary thing at Nantyr (our school) now, which is interesting.

Please see the official webpage for more information and to watch
