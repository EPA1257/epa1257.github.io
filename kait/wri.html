published writings

 

 

Sold (2019)

Sold is a weird collection of short fiction and nonfiction, nonsense, cardboard, a condom wrapper, and other things, very inspired by writings of Harmony Korine. The main theme was percieved friendship. This book and its accompanying film are no longer available for the time being, but will be republished at some point.

 

Mr. Hungry (2010)

Mr. Hungry is a kids book I wrote when I was 7 years old. My mom printed about 20-30 copies and we gave most of them away to people in my grade 1 class at the time. It's about a man who makes unhealthy eating decisions, but changes his ways by the end of the book. At some point I'll scan the manuscript, when I find it.

Kait Bell: A Life (2011)

A very silly attempt at an autobiography. Many details are embellished, some are not even true. Obviously not going to be printed any time soon, but it's very cutely and childishly narcissistic, you'll just have to take my word for it. Interestingly, there is nearly half a page dedicated to warning the reader not to feed chocolate to a dog.

 

Kait's Book - OK it's called a scrapbook! (2009-ish?)

 As the subtitle proclaims, this is a scrapbook containing various different images from my early childhood. If remember correctly, might have been a show-and-tell project. of course, due to containing private images, I don't plan on reproducing or reprinting this one any time soon, but it is worth mentioning to me.
