The Fucking Hate You: Bursting Out of My Skin


The Fucking Hate You does not exist. It never has and never will.
All of the music created by The Fucking Hate You was created using cgMusic by Maciej Biedrzycki - a Windows program that creates compositions using random number generation and preset rhythms/melodies.
The idea for The Fucking Hate You's album was inspired heavily by the themes of Today is the Day's "Sadness Will Prevail" (Relapse Records, 2002).

All music 100% created by a Windows 10 computer using cgMusic by Maciej Biedrzycki.
